01
Office Space (1999) Primary source · Comedy / dark comedy · 1h 29m

There is a scene from Office Space that describes Y2K and the debugging effort perfectly. Released before the year 2000, the adult comedy unintentionally serves as a valuable primary source for studying how Y2K work was understood during the era.

“I sit in a cubicle, and I update bank software for the 2000 switch. To save space they use two digits for the date instead of four, so like 98 instead of 1998. Uh, so I go through these thousands of lines of code and uh… it doesn’t really matter.”
